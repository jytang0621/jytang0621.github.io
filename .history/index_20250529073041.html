Jingyu Tang

HCI + NLP Researcher

I am currently supervised by Prof. Toby Li, and I will visit the US in person this summer. Previously, I was fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Juho Kim, Prof. Tianyi Zhang, and Prof. Yao Wan.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction and Natural Language Processing.

Specifically, I am interested in
(1) deepening our understanding of the existing gaps in aligning LLMs with human values and needs.
(2) designing and developing interactive systems that promote trust, safety, fairness and privacy protection, ultimately fostering alignment with humans.

I will apply for HCI PhD for 2026 Fall!
