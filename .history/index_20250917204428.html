Jingyu Tang

HCI + NLP Researcher

I am currently supervised by Prof. Toby Li, and I will visit the US in person this summer. Previously, I was fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Juho Kim, Prof. Tianyi Zhang, and Prof. Yao Wan.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction and Natural Language Processing.

Specifically, I am interested in
(1) Evaluation: I develop human-centered method to evaluate LLM-based agents beyond task success rates.
(2) Mitigation: I aim to build more reliable AI agents that align with human values and needs. This involves designing context-aware human-AI interaction paradigms for better human oversight and control, as well as exploring post-training methods that enhance agent robustness and protect users.

I will apply for HCI PhD for 2026 Fall!
