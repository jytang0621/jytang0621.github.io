Jingyu Tang

HCI + NLP Researcher

I am a research intern at Kixlab, supervised by Juho Kim. This summer, I will be joining SaNDwich Lab as a research intern under the supervision of Toby Li in this summmer. Previously, I interned at Bytedance as a frontend engineer.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction, Natural Language Processing, and Social Computing.

Specifically, I am interested in
(1) deepening our understanding of the existing gaps in aligning LLMs with human.
(2) designing and developing interactive systems that promote user trust and foster alignment with human.

I will be applying for PhD programs in HCI in Fall 2026!
